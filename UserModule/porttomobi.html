Porting to Mobi-Comm - FAQs

Find answers to common questions about porting your number

You can generate the Unique Porting Code (UPC) by sending an SMS with the text "PORT" followed by your 10-digit mobile number to 1900 from your current mobile connection. You'll receive an SMS with your UPC which is valid for 15 days.

The porting process typically takes 5-7 working days after you submit all required documents and information. You'll receive an SMS confirmation once the porting is complete. During this time, your current connection will remain active until the porting is finalized.

There will be a brief service interruption (usually less than 2 hours) when the porting is being completed. You'll receive an SMS notification about the exact timing. We recommend scheduling the porting during a time when you can afford a short service break.

You'll need:
  • Clear photocopy of your ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, etc.)
  • Clear photocopy of your address proof (if different from ID proof)
  • Passport size photograph
  • Current mobile number details
  • UPC code from your current operator

Yes, you can cancel your porting request within 24 hours of submission by calling our customer care at 1800-123-4567. After 24 hours, the porting process cannot be cancelled as it gets processed by the central MNP system.

Any unused balance or validity with your current operator will not be carried over to Mobi-Comm. We recommend using up your existing balance before the porting is completed. Once ported, you'll need to choose a new Mobi-Comm plan.
