Frequently Asked Questions

How do I recharge my Mobi-Comm number?

You can recharge your Mobi-Comm number by selecting a plan from our recharge options, entering your mobile number, and completing the payment through any of our secure payment methods.

How long does it take for the recharge to be activated?

Recharges are typically activated immediately after successful payment. In rare cases, it may take up to 5 minutes. You'll receive an SMS confirmation once your recharge is processed.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and Mobi-Comm wallet payments. You can also save your payment methods for faster checkout in the future.

Can I schedule a recharge for later?

Yes! You can schedule a recharge for any future date up to 30 days in advance. Just select your plan and choose "Schedule Recharge" instead of "Recharge Now".

What happens if I recharge while my current plan is still active?

If you recharge while your current plan is active, the new plan benefits will activate immediately after your current plan expires. You won't lose any unused benefits from your current plan.
