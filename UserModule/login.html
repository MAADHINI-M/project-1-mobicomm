Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

Activating your SIM is simple. Just insert it into your device and follow the on-screen instructions. Alternatively, you can visit our activation portal online or call our customer service for assistance. Activation typically completes within 15 minutes.

To port your number: 1) Generate a Porting Code (UPC) from your current provider, 2) Order a Mobi-Comm SIM, 3) Submit the porting request through our website or app, 4) Insert your new SIM when the porting completes (usually within 3 working days). Your service will continue uninterrupted during the process.

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay), UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking from over 50 banks, and auto-pay options for hassle-free recurring payments. Corporate customers can also request invoice billing.

Use our online coverage map by entering your address or pin code. For detailed signal strength information, download our network app which provides real-time coverage data. Our customer service can also provide specific information about your location's network quality.
